wafofig9

PURPOSE

Intensity of rainflow cycles computed from St

SYNOPSIS

wafofig9

DESCRIPTION 

  WAFOFIG9  Intensity of rainflow cycles computed from St
            through Markov approximation, compared with the 
            cycles found in the simulation.
