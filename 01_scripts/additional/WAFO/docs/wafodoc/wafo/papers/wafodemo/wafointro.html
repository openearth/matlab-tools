PURPOSE

Info about WAFODEMO: WAFO - a Matlab toolbox for analysis of random waves and loads

SYNOPSIS

wafointro

DESCRIPTION 

 WAFOINTRO  Info about WAFODEMO: WAFO - a Matlab toolbox for analysis of random waves and loads
 
  The files in this directory can reproduce the figures in the 
  article of 
  
  Brodtkorb, P.A., Johannesson, P., Lindgren, G., Rychlik, I., Ryden, J
  and Sjo, E. (2000)
  'WAFO - a Matlab toolbox for analysis of random waves and loads'
   to appear in Proceedings of 10th ISOPE Conference, Seattle
  
 
  The paper demonstrates in examples some of the capabilities of WAFO:
 
    - Construction of model spectra, directional model spectra with known 
      spreading function
    - Simulation of transformed Gaussian processes
    - Spectral density estimation
    - Probability distributions of wave characteristics:
         1) Joint density of Vcf and Ac
         2) Joint density of Tcf and Ac given Tc=5
         3) Distribution of extremal crest
    - Fatigue, Cycles and Markov models
 
    (See wafomenu for numenclature and wave parameter definitions)
 
  See also  wafomenu, recdemo
