wafofig10

PURPOSE

Intensity of trough-crest cycles computed from St

SYNOPSIS

wafofig10

DESCRIPTION 

  WAFOFIG10 Intensity of trough-crest cycles computed from  St
            through Markov approximation, compared with the 
            cycles found in the simulation.
