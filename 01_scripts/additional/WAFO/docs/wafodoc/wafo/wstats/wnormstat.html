wnormstat

PURPOSE

Mean and variance for the Normal distribution.

SYNOPSIS

[m,v]= wnormstat(m0,v0);

DESCRIPTION 

 WNORMSTAT Mean and variance for the Normal distribution.
  
  CALL:  [m,v] = wnormstat(m0,v0)

  m0, v0 = parameters of the Normal distribution.
