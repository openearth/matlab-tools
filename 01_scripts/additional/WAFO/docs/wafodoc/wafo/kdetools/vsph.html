vsph

PURPOSE

calculates volume of d-dimensional sphere with radius r0

SYNOPSIS

c=vsph(d,r0)

DESCRIPTION 

  VSPH calculates volume of  d-dimensional sphere with radius r0 
  
  CALL: vd = vsph(d,r0) 
  
   d  = dimension of sphere 
   r0 = radius of sphere (default 1)
